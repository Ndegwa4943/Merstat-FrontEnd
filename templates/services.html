Our Professional Services

Discover how our specialized services can help your organization achieve measurable results and sustainable growth through expert monitoring, evaluation, and research solutions.

Our Services

MONITORING AND EVALUATION

  • Impact Evaluation of projects/programs for organizations.
  • Design of project/program measurement frameworks (Logical frameworks, Results chains, Indicators of change, measurement plans, defining key assumptions, projections, etc.).
  • Application of Donor Committee for Enterprise Development (DCED) standards in measurement frameworks.
  • Development of attribution strategies for different programs.
  • Project/program data collection and reporting services for monitoring of activities on behalf of clients.
  • Advisory services on design, planning, implementation and monitoring of projects/programs.

RESEARCH AND STATISTICS

  • Baseline and Impact evaluation (midline & end line) assessments.
  • Data analysis services (Qualitative and Quantitative).
  • Development of research tools (Hard questionnaires, Open Data Kit-ODK, etc.).
  • Development of research plans using appropriate methodologies.
  • Market displacement studies such as conflict sensitivity analysis.
  • Commercial viability assessments.
  • Customer/consumer surveys.

CAPACITY BUILDING

  • Capacity building for organizations on different implementation approaches (M4P, Value chain, Supply chain, Leveraging Economic opportunities-LEO, etc.).
  • Capacity building for M&E teams on development of results measurement frameworks.
  • Capacity building for organizations on application of DCED standards.
  • Advanced excel training.
  • Capacity building for organizations on qualitative and quantitative data analysis using appropriate statistical software.
  • Statistical software training (SPSS, STATA, R, Epi-info, SAS, QDA miner).
  • Capacity building on design of research plans and research tools using technology.
