TRAININGS

MERSTAT strengthens capability through practical, hands on training. Every course includes ready to use templates, facilitation guides, job aids, and optional coaching. Delivery is available in person, virtual, or blended.

How our trainings work

Needs assessment and co design

Interactive sessions with real data and scenarios

Action plans and tool handover

Optional follow up clinics to embed practice

Flagship Tracks

DCED systems MEL

Outcome: Teams set up and operate DCED aligned systems that are ready for audit and useful for management.

Core modules:
  • Results chains and systemic boundaries, measurement plans and roles
  • AAER learning routines, data quality
  • Documentation for audit, pre audit readiness
Deliverables:
  • Results chain and indicator templates, measurement plan
  • AAER calendar, audit readiness checklist

MSD and MSR for program teams

Outcome: Teams design and implement market systems interventions that are resilient and measurable at scale.

Core modules:
  • System mapping, viable business models, facilitation tactics
  • Resilience signals, sentinel indicators, systemic change measurement
Deliverables:
  • Intervention guide pack with logic, indicators, and learning plan

Evaluations and evidence design

Outcome: Teams plan and commission credible evaluations that inform real decisions.

Core modules:
  • Rolling and retrospective baselines, midline learning reviews, endline assessments
  • Contribution analysis, counterfactual thinking, displacement checks, case studies and most significant change
Deliverables:
  • Evaluation ToR pack, sampling and power calculator
  • Analysis plan templates, reporting shells

Jobs measurement frameworks

Deliverables:
  • Jobs framework toolkit, data collection tools
  • Dashboard schema, guidance on multipliers

Formats, Audience, and Certification

Formats and Duration

  • Intensive masterclasses from one to three days
  • Modular academies across four to eight weeks
  • Executive clinics for senior leaders
  • Train the trainer pathways for internal sustainability
  • Embedded coaching during live implementation cycles

Audience and Language

Audience: Program teams, MEL and TPM staff, analysts and data engineers, fund managers and board members, government partners, and delivery teams.

Language: English by default. Local language options on request.

Certification

Participants receive a certificate of completion. Custom certificates are available when linked to internal competency frameworks or university partners.
