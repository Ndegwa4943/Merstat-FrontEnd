Methods We Apply When Appropriate

DCED Systems MEL

We build results chains that match business models and boundaries. We set measurement plans with clear indicators, roles and data sources.

Systemic Change Measurement

We track sentinel indicators and map market actor networks. We monitor adoption, adaptation, expansion and response.

Evaluations and Research

We design baselines as rolling or retrospective when timing requires flexibility. We deliver midline learning reviews and endline assessments.

Attribution & Counterfactuals

We link causal narratives to evidence. We estimate counterfactuals when feasible. We document assumptions and run sensitivity checks.

Remote Monitoring

We apply risk based sampling for phone follow up, call backs and audio audit. We use time stamped GPS and photo verification.

Health Systems Strengthening

We prepare routine health information systems for analysis and visualization. We provide decision dashboards for quality and coverage.

Climate MRV

We build activity data systems that field teams can maintain. We develop emissions reduction calculators that are verification ready.

ESG and Investment Tracking

We run ESG diagnostics and set reporting tools that fit operations. We train teams and boards to manage ESG through routine cycles.

Jobs and Inclusion

We design jobs frameworks for direct, indirect and induced employment. We estimate FTE with time based methods and track quality.

Assurance and Ethics

DCED compliance is built by design. Research ethics approvals are obtained where required. Data protection and safeguarding are applied consistently.
